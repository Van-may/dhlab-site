Where to Find Us

Located within the heart of Yale University, the Franke Family Digital Humanities Laboratory (DHLab) can be found just off the main entrance to Sterling Memorial Library. The DHLab maintains the same hours as the Library.

Consultations

The DHLab offers individual and group consultations for digital humanities projects at any stage of development. Staff address questions related to best practices, data management, corpus creation, visualizations, digital tools, and more. In addition to scheduled meetings, the DHLab also offers weekly DH Office Hours as another opportunity to discuss projects or general digital humanities ideas.
